Assistant Vice President for Technology Transfer

Welcome to the Office of Technology Transfer (OTT) at Rice University. We are the catalyst for transforming groundbreaking research into global impact. As the university’s innovation engine, we partner directly with Rice’s faculty, staff, and students to propel cutting-edge discoveries out of the lab and into the marketplace.

To our external partners, investors, and industry colleagues: A massive welcome to you. We want you to see Rice as your premier hub for innovation. Reach out, tap into what we are building here, and let’s partner to shape the future together.
